Have a Nice Death

Title : Have a Nice Death

Release Date : March 23, 2023

Have a Nice Death is a 2D action roguelike in which you play as Death on the verge of burnout, having to set his employees straight, before the balance of souls is compromised. You'll have to grab your scythe and show who's in charge to the recalcitrant staff.

About the game...

Welcome to Have a Nice Death 1.0 !

A big thank you to our community for our early access journey! Now you can experience the eighth department - the Department of Inevitable Time - with a new boss, mini-boss, enemies, storyline ending, "quality of death" upgrades, and more.

Welcome to Death Inc.

Discover and explore the more obscure, procedurally generated departments of Death Inc. and meet the colorful staff that occupy them, such as Pump Quinn, your dedicated intern and personal assistant, who is always quick to share the latest company gossip.

It is by reaping that one becomes the Reaper

Hone your skills in frantic hack n' slash combat. With over 70 unique weapons and spells to discover, you'll be able to create devastating combinations and upgrade them.

There is life after death

Since Death can't... die, use what you've learned over the course of your runs to defeat the many stubborn employees and bosses of Death, Inc. Each attempt will earn you a performance review interview and unlock items and upgrades that will help you achieve

your goals.

Death is full of surprises

Accepting a bonus is not always well received by the company culture. For example, choosing an upgrade in the form of a Curse may pique the curiosity of the Labor Inspector and incur the wrath of the staff, or worse: increase the price of kafe and other essential

items for the job at hand.

System requirements minimum
  • Operating System (OS) : Windows 10 (64 bits)
  • Processor : Intel Core i3 6100
  • RAM : 4 GB of memory
  • Graphics : NVIDIA GeForce GTX 650 or higher | AMD R7 360 or higher
  • DirectX : Version 10
  • Disk space : 2 GB of available disk space
  • Additional notes : Specifications are not final and are subject to change. Intel HD may crash.
